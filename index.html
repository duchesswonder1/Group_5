Welcome to the SMACK Library

The library at St. Mary's College Kisubi (SMACK) plays an important role in promoting academic excellence and supporting the school's mission of providing quality education. It serves as a central hub for learning and research, offering students an environment that encourages intellectual growth and academic exploration. Through its wide range of resources and facilities, the library helps learners acquire knowledge and develop essential research skills needed for their studies.

One of the major strengths of the library is the extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources that cater to various subjects taught at both O-level and A-level. The inctitution provides students with access to valuable learning materials that support their curriculum and enhance their understanding of different disciplines. In addition, the library offere weel-designed spaces that accommodate both the individual and group study sessions, ensuring that students have a comfortable and conducive atmosphere for learning.

The library also emphasises the integration of technology into education. Equipped with modern computers and high-speed internet connectivity, it enables students to conduct online research efficiently and access digital learning materials with ease. These technological facilities improve the quality of learning by exposing students to up-to-date information and providing opprtunities for independent study and academic advancement.

The Higher School Certificate (HSC) Library specifically serves students in the senior secondary section and is dedicated to meeting their advanced academic needs. it was officially opened by the late Hon. Namirembe Butamazire, who was the Minister of State in charge of primary education at that time. The establishment of this library was intended to enrich the learning experience of advanced-level students by providing them with specialised resources and a suitable environment for higher academic achievement.

Another important facility is the SMACK Memorial Library, which holds both historical and educational significance. Established in 1964 and commissioned by Lord Snowdon, the library was built in memory of twelve students who tragically died in a motor accident. it stands as a symbol of the colege's resilience and commitment to preserving its rich history. Besides serving as a tribute to the fallen students, the Memorial Library continues to support the school's academic programs by providing students and staff with a large collection of books, journals, and other learning materials, thereby contributing significantly to the educational development of the institution.
